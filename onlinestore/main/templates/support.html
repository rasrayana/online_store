    &FAQS

    Here is a list of the most frequently asked questions.
    HOW DO I MAKE A PURCHASE? ✦

    ✦Shopping the Vivienne Westwood online boutique is easy: if you know what you are looking for, use the specific category links, such as 'shoes', 'accessories' shop by ‘trends’ section on the site, or through our 'icons'. Once you have found an item, select your size, and colour and use the 'add to bag' button underneath. Review the items in your shopping bag by selecting the 'shopping bag' link on the page. From the shopping bag you can directly edit product details (colour, sizes, and quantities), remove them in case you’ve changed your mind or add products to your wish list. Click on 'checkout' to complete your order.

    HOW TO PAY FOR AN ORDER? ✦

    ✦We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, MasterCard Debit, Switch/Maestro, Visa Delta payments and PayPal.
    ✦You can add your card info during the payment step on checkout. All payments are secure.

    DO I NEED AN ACCOUNT TO PLACE AN ORDER? ✦

    ✦Online orders placed using guest checkout cannot be added to registered accounts. Setting up an account is not mandatory, but if you register, you will be able to enjoy the following benefits through your 'My Account':
    • Track your orders and review past purchases,
    • Gain access to your ‘Wish List’,
    • Be notified when new products are available
    • Save your address and card details for faster shopping,
    • Manage your account details and newsletter subscriptions.

    HOW DO I MAKE A PURCHASE? ✦

    ✦Vivienne Westwood sizes vary from label to label; we recommend checking the 'Size Guide' link which can be found on the product pages underneath the size selector. If you are still unsure and would like to discuss our sizing with a member of our team, please contact our Customer Service by emailing coqcore_service@viviennewestwood.com .

    CAN I CHANGE MY SHIPPING ADRESS AFTER THE ORDER HAS BEEN DISPATCHED? ✦

    ✦Once an online order is placed, we are unable to redirect orders to a different address for security purposes. Therefore, please ensure you provide a suitable shipping address for the specified delivery times. Alternatively – You can contact the carrier provider directly for further assistance should you be unavailable to receive your delivery as scheduled.
